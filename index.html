About

Mining strategist.
Market analyst. Trusted advisor.

I am a strategist and consultant with nearly a decade of experience advising mining companies, institutional investors, and governments across the full spectrum of metals and critical minerals — with a core focus on copper and lithium, and deep coverage across base metals, battery materials, gold, rare earths, and the broader energy transition commodities.

As Director of Metals & Mining for the Americas at Wood Mackenzie, I lead commercial growth, complex client engagements, and thought leadership across North and Latin America. My work spans investment decisions, market intelligence, and strategic positioning for the companies and capitals shaping the next economy.

Chilean-born, New York-based. Fluent in English and Spanish. Regularly quoted in major Latin American business press on commodity markets and mining strategy.
